TogetherSafe

Your group. Your night. Your safety.

TogetherSafe keeps your circle connected and protected during nights out, road trips, festivals, and everyday life. Create a quick profile, form a group, and everyone can see each other’s live location, distance, and SOS alerts in real time.

To unlock full access, TogetherSafe runs on a simple $5/month subscription that keeps the servers live, the maps updating, and your group protected.

After creating your profile, you’ll be prompted to activate your $5/month subscription.
